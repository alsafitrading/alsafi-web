Solutions

Retail Software solutions

POS, inventory, and barcode tools for department stores, supermarkets, restaurants, and back-office teams — with demos, hardware options, and training across UAE & Oman.

From busy supermarket lanes to full-line department stores, table-service restaurants, and cyclical stock counts, we help you keep checkout fast, stock accurate, and shelf labels consistent. Contact us for a tailored package.

Department store POS

Handle multiple departments, brands, and price rules in one system. Support promotions, returns, exchanges, and staff permissions so floor teams and cashiers stay efficient during peak weekends and seasonal sales.

Supermarket POS

Built for high transaction volumes: quick item lookup, barcode scanning, mix-and-match offers, and smooth integration with retail scales where needed. Keep queues short and end-of-day reconciliation straightforward.

Restaurant POS

Manage dine-in, takeaway, and counter service with table plans, course firing, modifiers, and split payments. Kitchen-friendly order views help reduce errors and speed up service during rush hours.

Stock taking

Run full or partial counts with mobile or dedicated devices, freeze periods where required, and export variances for finance and buyers. Repeatable workflows make cyclical counts less disruptive to trading.

Barcode printing

Design shelf labels, product stickers, and carton tags with the right symbology for your supply chain. Align printing with your item master so stores and warehouses scan the same codes without relabeling chaos.
